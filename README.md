# GTA VI Global Pricing Tracker

Live PlayStation Store pricing for Grand Theft Auto VI across 73 regions worldwide.

**[View the interactive dashboard](https://jishnuteegala.github.io/gta-vi-global-pricing/)**

## What this tracks

- Standard Edition and Ultimate Edition prices in local currency and US$ equivalent
- Price variance against the US baseline ($79.99 Standard / $99.99 Ultimate)
- 62 live regions, 0 pending, 11 unavailable

## Key findings

- **Cheapest**: South Korea at US$58.19 (27.3% below US baseline)
- **Priciest**: Israel at US$107.09 (33.9% above US baseline)
- Exchange rates snapshot: June 25, 2026 at 12:15 PM UTC via Google Finance
- Prices may include local taxes (VAT/GST) depending on region

## Project structure

- `index.html` — Interactive single-page dashboard (Chart.js, Tailwind CSS)
- `style.css` — Extracted stylesheet
- `assets/bg.jpg` — Background artwork (Rockstar Games)
- `assets/images/` — Generated chart PNGs with @jishnuteegala watermarks
